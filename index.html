Places you should visit in the Philippines! Luzon ver.

Luzon sits at the northern end of the Philippines, and is the most populated island along with being the country's largest.
It is widely known for it's mountains, coral reefs and beaches.

Luzon is also known due to it's big number in Tourist Attractions.
Here are a few that I think you would like!

Number One: Vigan City!

Located at the west coast of Luzon and is well known for it's well-preserved Spanish Colonial and Asian Architechtures,
Vigan City is a World-Heitage Site that has one of the few last Hispanic Towns in the Philippines.

A visit to Vigan City is like going on a trip back in time to Hispanic Era due to it's Spanish-Inspired Architectures.
Other than being notorious for it's spanish-inspired framework, Vigan City is also very widely known for it's many other tourist spots such as;

" Calle Crisologo "

It is Vigan's most popular tourist attraction. It's cobblestone streets,horse-drawn carriages and rustic mansions are quite favored by many.

" Pagburnayan Jar Factory "

A very amazing thing to note when taking a trip to the City is that you also learn about it's history and culture, Pagburnayan Jar Factory is one of the very few Jar Factories left in Vigan. It is a very popular must-try activity.

" Mindoro Beach "

A beach where it's sand is the color black, but despite that; It had a very smooth and fine texture. It also gives a front-view of The West Philippine Sea.
Tourists can take tips, but only with caution. The current is quite strong, after all. Mindoro Beach is also a place where several people can watch sunsets.
